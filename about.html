About IdiomKu

A fun way to explore the richness of Malay idioms.

Hi there! I’m Julian, the creator of IdiomKu. A few years ago, I spent some time studying Bahasa Malaysia and interning at Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka (DBP), Malaysia's Institute of Language and Literature. I quickly noticed how many idioms and expressions Malaysians use in everyday conversation—idioms that don’t always translate directly but offer refreshing insights into Malaysian culture.

Rather than creating a rigorous learning program, I wanted to build something fun and accessible. IdiomKu is a collection of categorized idioms that you can browse and quiz yourself on in your free time. Think of it as a cultural deep dive, one idiom at a time.

Meet Sang, our mascot! Sang is a clever mousedeer, inspired by the cleverness of people who masterfully use idioms in their daily lives. If I find the time, I’ll create more projects, like a guide to mastering Bahasa’s tricky imbuhan grammar forms. Stay tuned!
